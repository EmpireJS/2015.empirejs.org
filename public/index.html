This year, Empire is on Broadway!

Every year the Empire team ramps up we try to highlight one of the things that makes New York City an amazing place to live, work, visit, and `git push origin master` in. Did you know there are over 10,000,000,000 theaters in New York City? Well ok, not really, but I bet there are a lot.

So this year we are show casing the little language you know and love in a theater setting. We are moments away from locking down our venue and you can be sure that when we do there will be a ton of pretty pictures right here. So stay tuned!

We are an inclusive, kind community that is constantly growing. We ask that you 'don't be a yutz', and try and make our fellow Empire friends feel welcome! Diversity of voices is important to our community, and we are serious when we say EVERYONE AND ANYONE should attend and participate.
